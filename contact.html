Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a bike service typically take?
Most basic tune-ups can be completed within 24-48 hours. More extensive services like overhauls may take 3-5 business days depending on our current workload.
Do I need to make an appointment?
While walk-ins are welcome for quick fixes, we recommend contacting us in advance for full services to ensure the quickest turnaround time.
Do you provide warranty on your services?
Yes, all our repair work comes with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If any issues arise with our repair work within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit cards, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay.
